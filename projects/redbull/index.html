Red Bull Creation

Red Bull Creation is an annual maker, inventor, and hacker competition. It uses a digital format to pit the best teams across the country against one another, culminating in a 72-hour build-off. To reach the final round, each team had to submit a video of their creation.

The only rules for the contest? Have fun and build something awesome.

Little Tinker + Red Bull

When we got the invitation to compete, we put our hard hats together to answer one question: "How do we hook this build up to the Internet?"

The result? A Twitter-powered Red Bull Machine.

The way it works is simple: Tweet using the hashtag #GiveMeWings, and the machine dispenses, opens, and pours a Red Bull into glasses for you and your friends to enjoy.

The goal was to delight and inspire. Using the latest in Wi-Fi technology and cutting-edge fabrication tools, the project does just that by turning an everyday task into something extraordinary.
