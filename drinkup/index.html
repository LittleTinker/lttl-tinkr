#DrinkUp


The Twitter Powered Drinking Game.

we you.

INSPIRATION:

At Little Tinker the itch to always be building something is weaved into our very existance. If there is one other thing you can count on in the office it is our love of good times and good friends. One night when these two passions converged, the next morning, a global social experience emerged in #DrinkUp, the twitter powered drinking game.

WHAT IS IT?

The premise of the game is simple tweet #DrinkUp, sound of a can cracking plays, and when you hear the sound take a drink! Play with your friends in your living room while playing with your friends across the country simlutaneously.
